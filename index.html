What is this?

This is AliceBlue.com, a mock-up website created by Naomi Massey, in order to play around with HTML. It features the full text of Lewis Carroll's Alice's Adventures in Wonderland.


What is AliceBlue?

AliceBlue can refer to #9CD6E4 (the background for this website!) or #F0F8FF (the colour you see behind this text!). In origin, it actually has more to do with Theodore Roosevelt then Alice's iconic blue dress.

The lighter version was included in the 1987 X11 colours, used in early web development.

You can learn more about AliceBlue (the colour) here!

Who is Lewis Carroll?

Lewis Carroll (1832 - 1898) was an English author and poet, most famous for his stories Alice's Adventures in Wonderland and Alice Through the Looking-Glass.

Carroll's works entered into the public domain in 1907.

If you haven't read Alice's Adventures in Wonderland, you can do so on this site! (and elsewhere...)
